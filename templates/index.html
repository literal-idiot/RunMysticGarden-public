API Documentation

Features

User Management

Secure registration and authentication with JWT tokens. Email/password login system.

Run Tracking

Log runs with distance, duration, and intensity. Automatic pace calculation and progress tracking.

Coin Rewards

Earn coins based on distance and intensity. Bonus rewards for milestone achievements.

Mystical Seeds

Purchase seeds with earned coins. Different rarities and plant types with unique requirements.

Plant Growth

Plants grow based on your running activity. Different stages from seed to blooming flower.

Garden Management

Customize your mystical garden. Level up to expand and unlock new features.

Strava Integration

Strava OAuth integration is planned for future releases. Currently, runs must be logged manually through the API. The authentication endpoint is available at /auth/strava/connect but returns a "not implemented" status.
